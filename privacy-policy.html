Privacy Policy

How TEDA collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Home Privacy Policy

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Teso Elites Development Association (TEDA) collects, uses, and protects information submitted through our website, including our membership, application, volunteer, partner, and contact forms. It also fulfils the disclosure requirements of Web3Forms, the service TEDA uses to process form submissions.

Last updated: January 2026  |  Questions: tedayouthteso@gmail.com

What Information We Collect

When you submit any form on this website, we collect the information you provide directly, which may include your name, email address, phone number, district, school or institution, gender, and any message or motivation text you write. We do not collect payment information, as TEDA does not process donations or payments through this website.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our forms is used solely to process your membership registration, position application, volunteer or partnership interest, or general inquiry, and to respond to you by email or WhatsApp. Approved member names and school or institution may be shown in a public member directory, as described in our Terms and Conditions.

Form Processing via Web3Forms

Form submissions on this site are delivered using Web3Forms, a third party form processing service, directly to TEDA's official email address. Web3Forms processes the data solely to deliver your submission to us and does not use it for any other purpose. We recommend reviewing Web3Forms' own privacy practices if you would like further detail on their processing.

How We Protect Your Information

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the information you submit, including domain restricted form keys, spam filtering, and secure hosting. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information held by TEDA. You may also request removal from the public member directory at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at tedayouthteso@gmail.com.

Information From Minors

Some TEDA programs involve secondary school students. Where a form is completed by a minor, we recommend a parent or guardian review the submission. We do not knowingly collect more information from minors than is necessary for the stated purpose of the form.

Changes to This Policy

TEDA may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Significant changes will be communicated through our official channels. Continued use of this website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions About Your Data?

Reach out any time, we are happy to explain exactly how your information is used.
