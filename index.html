Empowering Youth.Preserving Culture. Driving Change.

Teso Elites Development Association (TEDA) unites and equips young people to become leaders of education, culture, innovation, and sustainable development across the Teso sub region of Eastern Uganda.

Our Vision

An empowered generation of Teso youth driving education, culture, and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To empower the youth of Teso to lead in education, preserve culture, and champion sustainable development for a thriving future.

Our Promise

TEDA is more than an organization, it is a movement of young people determined to shape a brighter future for the Teso region.

What We Do

Our Focus Areas

We drive practical, measurable impact across six key areas that matter most to Teso youth and communities.

View All
📚

Education & Mentorship

Supporting vulnerable students, reducing school dropouts, and linking youth to scholarship and skilling opportunities through mentorship programs.

Learn more
🌿

Climate Change

Empowering young people to become climate actors and sustainable farmers through practical training, tree planting, and environmental awareness.

Learn more
🏥

Health & Well being

Community health talks, sanitation and hygiene campaigns, malaria prevention awareness, and formation of school and community health clubs.

Learn more
💡

Entrepreneurship

Equipping youth with business skills, financial literacy, and a mindset of job creation rather than job seeking to build sustainable futures.

Learn more
🏛️

Youth Leadership & Civic Engagement

Training young leaders in governance and accountability, amplifying youth voices in district and national forums and decision making spaces.

Learn more
🎭

Cultural Preservation

Promoting unity, identity, and pride in Teso heritage while strengthening intergenerational dialogue between youth and elders.

Learn more
