Ten Ways In

Opportunities Open to Members

01

Scholarships & Bursaries

Support and linkage to scholarship opportunities for vulnerable and high potential learners, so school fees never have to be the reason a young person's education ends.

02

Mentorship Programs

One on one and group mentorship from experienced volunteers, guiding members through school, career, and personal development challenges.

03

Leadership Positions

Real responsibility within TEDA's structure, from zonal coordination to executive roles, giving members hands on governance and management experience.

04

Youth Forum Access

Priority access to the annual Teso Youth Forum, where members dialogue directly with leaders and policymakers on education, climate, health, and accountability.

05

Networking with Professionals

Direct connection to TEDA's directors, ambassadors, elders, and partner organizations, building relationships that open doors long after any single event ends.

06

Community Project Participation

Hands on involvement in real initiatives, tree planting, health outreach, entrepreneurship drives, giving members practical experience and visible community impact.

07

Skilling & Training Workshops

Practical vocational and business training, including tailoring, soap making, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship, built for members who want income generating skills.

08

Certificate of Participation

Recognized certificates for completed trainings, workshops, and volunteer service, giving members documentation of their growth and contribution.

09

Ambassador Program

A path to represent TEDA within your school, institution, or community as an Institutional Ambassador, championing the mission and expanding TEDA's reach.

10

Digital Skills Training

Basic to intermediate digital literacy sessions, equipping members with the computer and online skills needed for today's job market and further study.
