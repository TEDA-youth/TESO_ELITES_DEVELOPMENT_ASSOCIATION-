Our Six Focus Areas

At TEDA, we focus on practical and measurable impact across key areas that address the most pressing challenges facing young people and communities in the Teso sub region.

Focus Area 1

Education & Mentorship

TEDA champions education as a cornerstone for personal growth and community development. Through mentorship programs and community sensitization, we guide and support young people to stay in school, overcome challenges, and remain committed to their education.

By empowering youth with knowledge, guidance, and encouragement, we help reduce school dropout rates and nurture a generation capable of shaping a brighter future for themselves and their communities, even as long term goals like scholarships remain part of our vision for the future.

Mentorship programs and leadership training
Supporting vulnerable students to reduce school dropouts
Community sensitization on the value of education
Linking youth to scholarship and skilling opportunities

Education & Mentorship

Focus Area 2

Climate Change

The issue to be addressed is the urgency of climate adaptation among young people in climate vulnerable communities. Children are bearing the brunt of the impacts of climate change, and estimates show over 80% of injuries and deaths occurring due to climate change are in children, particularly those living in poor areas.

With the increasing impacts of climate change on water availability and food security, children and youth are particularly affected. Their rights to survive, learn, grow and flourish are directly threatened. Educating and empowering children from vulnerable communities can transform their capacity and reduce their vulnerability, and our team is empowering young people to become climate actors and sustainable farmers.

We have learnt the importance of practical training, not just education, so that young people are upskilled with climate adaptation strategies that strengthen their resilience and position them as powerful climate actors, while integrating local resources and knowledge to challenge the perception that sustainable agriculture is expensive to adopt.

Tree planting initiatives across communities
Green housing promotion
Environmental awareness programs
Practical climate adaptation training
Sustainable farming and agriculture skills

Climate Change

Focus Area 3

Health & Well being

TEDA prioritizes health promotion and disease prevention as a foundation for strong and productive communities. Through community health talks, sanitation and hygiene sensitization, malaria prevention awareness, and the formation of school and community health clubs, we empower people with knowledge to protect themselves and their families.

We believe that preventing disease, improving hygiene practices, and strengthening health awareness are essential steps toward reducing avoidable illnesses and building a healthier, more resilient population.

Community health talks and sensitization
Sanitation and hygiene awareness campaigns
Malaria prevention and education
Formation of school and community health clubs
Reproductive health education programs

Health & Well being

Focus Area 4

Entrepreneurship

TEDA promotes entrepreneurship as a sustainable pathway to economic empowerment and community transformation. We equip young people, especially those Not in Employment, Education, or Training (NEET), with practical vocational skills and structured business training in areas such as financial literacy, business planning, and innovation.

By nurturing a mindset of job creation rather than job seeking, we enable youth to build small enterprises that generate income, strengthen local economies, and create lasting social impact. For international partners, supporting TEDA's entrepreneurship initiatives means investing in grassroots innovation, poverty reduction, and youth led sustainable development.

Practical vocational skills training (tailoring, soap making)
Financial literacy and business planning
Innovation and creative problem solving
Entrepreneurship mentorship programs
Support for youth led startup businesses

Entrepreneurship

Focus Area 5

Youth Leadership & Civic Engagement

TEDA trains young leaders in governance, accountability, and civic responsibility so they can represent their communities with confidence. We work to amplify youth voices in district and national forums, ensuring young people have a genuine seat at decision making tables rather than a symbolic one.

Our flagship platform for this work is the annual Teso Youth Forum, which brings youth, elders, and policymakers together for dialogue. Alongside it, we run civic education sessions on youth rights and responsibilities, preparing a generation of leaders who understand both how governance works and how to hold it accountable.

Training young leaders in governance and accountability
Amplifying youth voices in district and national forums
Organizing the annual Teso Youth Forum
Civic education and youth rights awareness
Youth representation in decision making spaces

Youth Leadership

Focus Area 6

Cultural Preservation

TEDA promotes culture and identity, recognizing that sustainable development must respect and preserve the values, traditions, and heritage of the people it serves. We value the rich culture and traditions of Teso as a foundation for identity and pride.

Preserving culture connects youth to their roots while preparing them for the future. It also strengthens intergenerational dialogue between elders and young people, ensuring that the wisdom of generations past is carried forward. Through cultural events, dialogue sessions, and awareness programs, TEDA ensures Teso heritage remains a living part of community life, not just a memory of the past.

Promoting unity and pride in Teso heritage
Strengthening intergenerational dialogue
Cultural events and awareness programs
Documenting and celebrating Teso traditions
Connecting youth to their cultural identity

Cultural Preservation
