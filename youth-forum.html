About the Forum

Teso Youth Forum

The Teso Youth Forum is an annual gathering that brings together young people from different districts and regions to engage in meaningful dialogue about the issues affecting their lives and communities. It serves as a youth parliament, a safe and structured platform where young people sit side by side with leaders, policymakers, and stakeholders to openly discuss policies, governance, development challenges, and practical solutions.

Held once every year, the Forum creates space for youth voices to be heard, respected, and considered in decision making processes. It encourages constructive dialogue between generations, strengthens civic engagement, and promotes accountability and transparency in leadership.

The Teso Youth Forum is more than just a meeting, it is a movement that empowers young people to actively participate in shaping policies, advocate for their rights, and take responsibility as leaders of today.
